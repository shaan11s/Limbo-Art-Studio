About Us

Limbo Art Studio is a contemporary art space dedicated to exploring the boundaries between reality and imagination. Our collection features works that challenge perception and invite viewers into contemplative spaces.

Our studio serves as both a gallery and a creative laboratory, where artists are encouraged to experiment with new forms and push the boundaries of their practice.
